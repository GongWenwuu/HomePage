  • Low Rank Tensor Learning Methods and Its Applications on ITS

    • Project Page I would like to say, the Methods and Applications is very cool!

    Our proposal is built upon modern techniques such as low-rank tensor learning, high dimensional vector autoregressive model, Bayesian factorization model, and subspace learning. We unify the domain of tensor methods and spatiotemporal data analysis and shows the practicability of the methods on large-scale traffic data tasks, which is an essential step towards learning from real-world transportation networks.

  • Dynamic Risk Assessment of Compound Hazards

    • Due to the interrelations between multiple hazards, the risk assessment of compound hazards faces several challenges VFS-IEM-IDM: (1) the evaluation of hazard level needs to consider the correlations between compound hazards drivers, (2) usually only a small number of data samples areavailable for estimating the joint probability distribution of the compound hazard drivers and the loss caused by the hazards, (3) the risk assessment process often ignores the temporal dynamics of compound hazard occurrences.
